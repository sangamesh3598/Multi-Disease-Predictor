malaria
Diagnostic

A disease caused by a plasmodium parasite, transmitted by the bite of infected mosquitoes. The severity of malaria varies based on the species of plasmodium. Symptoms are chills, fever and sweating, usually occurring a few weeks after being bitten. People travelling to areas where malaria is common typically take protective drugs before, during and after their trip..

Symptoms

  • 1. Chills
  • 2. Fever
  • 3. Fatigue
  • 4.Night sweats
  • 5.Shivering
  • 6.Sweating

Prevention

  • 1. Drape mosquito netting over beds.
  • 2. Wear long pants and long sleeves to cover your skin.
  • 3. Appropriate treatment

Treatment

  • 1. Medication to eliminate the parasite from the bloodstream
  • 2. Main antimalarial drugs are:
    1. * chloroquine
    2. * hydroxychloroquine
    3. * primaquine
    4. * artemisinin-based therapy
